This 3-way torchiere comes complete with the bulbs for only $5.00! The lamp features (1) 18-watt GU24 CFL and (1) 26-watt GU24 CFL. The 18-watt GU24 is the first click and provides the equivalent of a standard 75-watt incandescent bulb. The 26-watt GU24 is the second click and provides the equivalent of a standard 100-watt incandescent bulb. The third click uses both bulbs together, using 45-watts of power and giving the equivalent output of 175-watts.
